TYPES OF LOAN DOCUMENTS

  • Loan Application: This is common to all loans and will have basic details like your name, contact information, and loan amount.

  • Proof of Identity and Address: You'll typically need to provide a copy of your Aadhaar card, PAN card, Voter ID, driving license, or passport.

  • Income Proof: This will vary depending on whether you're salaried or self-employed. Salaried individuals might need salary slips, bank statements, or salary certificates. Self-employed individuals might need Income Tax Returns (ITRs) or business bank statements.

  • Loan-Specific Documents: On top of the above, there might be additional documents depending on the loan type. For example, a home loan might require property papers, while a gold loan might require proof of gold ownership.
