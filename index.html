Answer: You can improve your credit score by paying bills on time, keeping credit card balances low, and avoiding opening too many new accounts.

What is the best way to start investing for beginners?

Answer: Beginners can start investing by researching investment options, setting clear goals, and considering low-risk options such as mutual funds or index funds.

What documents do I need to open a bank account?

Answer: To open a bank account, you typically need proof of identity (such as a driver's license or passport) and proof of address (such as a utility bill or lease agreement).

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity on my bank account?

Answer: If you suspect fraudulent activity on your bank account, you should immediately contact your bank to report the issue, freeze your account if necessary, and monitor your accounts for any unauthorized transactions.
